Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Vylinkia Invest. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy informs you about how we handle your data when you use our mobile application and website.

We collect a minimum amount of data to ensure the proper functioning of the application:

  • Device identifiers (for push notifications).

3. Data Usage

Your data is used solely to:

  • Provide you with trading signals and alerts.

4. Account Deletion

In compliance with GDPR, you have the right to delete your account and all your personal data at any time. You can perform this action directly in the mobile application via Settings > Delete Account, or by contacting us via email.
